Why It Matters

Why Practices Are Switching to Outsourced Billing

Managing billing in-house pulls your team away from patient care. The practices that make the switch almost always say the same thing: they wish they'd done it sooner.

01

You're Losing Money on Denials

The average claim denial rate is around 20%. Most in-house billing teams can't appeal every one. A specialist company does — it's their full-time job.

02

In-House Costs More Than You Think

Salary, benefits, training, turnover — an in-house biller costs $45,000–$65,000/year. Billing companies charge 3–8% of collections and only get paid when you do.

03

Your Team's Time Has a Better Use

Every hour on billing paperwork is an hour not spent on patients. Outsourcing gives your staff that time back — and typically improves collection rates.

Everything You Want to Know

How does Get Billing Quotes work?
+

Fill out a short form about your practice — specialty, size, current billing setup. We match you with 3 vetted billing companies that fit your profile, and they reach out to you directly. The whole form takes about 2 minutes. You're not committing to anything.

Is there a cost to use this service?
+

No — it's completely free for practices. The billing companies pay to be part of our vetted network, not you. You'll never receive an invoice or be asked for a credit card.

Will I get spammed by a dozen companies?
+

No. You'll only hear from the 3 companies we match you with — no more. We don't sell your information or pass it to any third parties outside of your matched providers.

Do I have to switch billing companies?
+

Not at all. A lot of practices just want to know if they're getting a good deal. Compare the quotes, ask questions, and decide in your own time. No obligation, no pressure.

How are billing companies vetted?
+

Every company in our network has been verified for specialty experience, US-based operations, HIPAA compliance standards, and track record of performance. We review denial rates, client retention, and billing accuracy before bringing a company onto the platform.

What specialties do you support?
+

We support 60+ specialties including Internal Medicine, Cardiology, Orthopedics, Dermatology, Behavioral Health, Pediatrics, OB/GYN, Psychiatry, Neurology, Physical Therapy, and many more. Fill out the form and we'll confirm what's available for your field.
